Global STD Magazine

A quarterly magazine project with digital distribution and limited printed editions for Global STD, a certification and trainning house of international standars. The goal was to explain international standards in detail but in a simple way to the users (internal auditors and entrepeneurs), so they could use the magazine as a recurring element of consultation.

With all this technical information, the hardest part was to understand how to clarify. The solution was graphs, infographics, images and comparative tables of information.

However, at some point I felt it was too much text, so I decided to transform it into lists, new tables, emphasis text, and icons. I discover playing with the layout, white spaces and color was another way to make it readable and did each article unique and fun to design.

As part of the branding of Global STD and with the magazine's success, the team create a blog with a section that included infographics and demonstrative tables to connect easily with new users, and increase their Trainning sells.
