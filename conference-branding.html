Food Quality and Safety National Conference Branding

This National Conference’s aim was to exchange knowledge and experiences in terms of Food Quality and Safety. The new Branding had 2 goals. First, to seek the sponsorship of certified companies related to the Conference theme. And Second, to position Global STD as a leader and capable Certification House that works closely with the most recognized international organizations.

Creating the logo was the most challenged part of this project. I used 3 main concepts: quality, food and certification. The hard part was to integrate the 3 concepts in one isotype logo without having to much elements. The clue at the end was the negative space, I was so proud of this solution after all that sketching!

The easiest answer for the Food theme color pallete was warm colors, right? Well, this branding needed to project authority and leadership, so I went completely to the other side of the wheel: purple, indigo and blue. After that, everything went smoothly.

I also decided to play with a star and an hexagon to emphasize the certification and quality concepts. The result was a new texture that spiced things up with gradients and lines.
