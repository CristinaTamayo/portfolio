Allmoods Bakery UI/UX

Allmoods Bakery went from serving friends and family to selling crafts and community events. Their competitive difference is their flavours, which are based on moods, so if you are having a baby shower you can get the excited cake pop, or if you are celebrating a divorce you can get the salty devil food cake.

The goal was creating a brand and website that represents the uniqueness of their services, but also a capable brand to offer its products massively.The personality of the brand is elegant, joyous and fun. They value empathy and try to understand all their clients by having a wide variety of products so everyone can feel identified.

After doing a research of the user personas and the competence, I decided to stand out designing the website and branding neutral to reach both females and males.

I elaborated a mind map to analyze the primary and secondary audience, this aid me to create the first ideas for the website. I founded difficult to explain the user there was a product for each mood. Are they really going to buy a cake based on this? After some testing with user personas, I finally decided to use a filter search to choose the mood or type of event and get different product suggestions.

I didn´t used a template for the design system, of course I expend more time in this, but I did learn a lot of atomic elements!

The most interesting part was to do Sign in / Sign up prototype in Figma, I learned about user flows and prototyping.

To be honest, I changed the heading typography at the end, I thought it was too fluid for a website, and the thickness wasn’t really helping the contrast and accessibility requirements.
